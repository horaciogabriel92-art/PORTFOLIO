The Challenge

The client needed a brand presence fast. Less than 24 hours from concept to deploy. The solution? A Single-File React App — everything in one HTML file, React via CDN, Babel compiling JSX in the browser.

Post-pitch, we migrated to Astro for production. Zero JS by default, partial hydration with React islands where needed, and 100/100 Lighthouse scores.
